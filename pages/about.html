OUR STORY

Built on Trust, Driven by Purpose

Toff Homes was founded with a single mission: to make property ownership accessible, transparent, and rewarding for every Nigerian. We started in Lagos and have since expanded to Ibadan, Abuja, and beyond.

We are not just selling land we are building communities. Every estate we develop comes with roads, water, electricity, drainage, and security already in place before the first plot is sold.

Today, over 2,500 families call a Toff Homes estate their home, and we are just getting started.
